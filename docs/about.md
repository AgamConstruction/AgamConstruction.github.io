## Our Story

Agam Construction began its journey as a general works contractor in 2001.
Over the years, the company has undertaken numerous challenging projects,
amassing a wealth of skills, expertise, and experience in design-and-build
solutions, project management services, building trades, and related
engineering works.

Today, Agam Construction stands as a trusted main contractor for small to
medium-sized projects, delivering exceptional project management services
to coordinate specialist trades for industrial and commercial projects.
We offer innovative design inputs and engineering solutions, providing
value-added services that cater to the unique needs of our clients.
